ABOUT US

Our primary purpose is to let people access one place where they can find everything they would need to cook their favorite dishes or even try to make new dishes. You can access recipes, the ingredients needed, substitutes, best places you can buy items required in the recipe as well as reviews about the recipe including other people’s experiences with it!
You can look for recipes based on the ingredients you have, cuisine preferences and dietary and time restrictions. You can also use this site to look for the best nearby place to buy missing items. Finally, you can also rate and view other people’s reviews of a particular recipe.
